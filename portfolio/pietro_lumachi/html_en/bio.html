Cv/bio

Pietro Lumachi is an italian , figurative, self taught oil painter. His works are very well known in many collections throughout Europe, but not only, in 2006 was invited to exhibit at the National Museum of fine Art in Caracas by the Venezoelan Ministry of Culture. Elegance, mistery and a cocktail of emotions concour to form his work:

Lumachi admires many contemporary Masters such as Freud, Jennings, Vettriano although he mantains the culture and the soul of his native land, never forgetting the Renaissance and Baroque: Lumachi is also a remarkable portraiter: He lives and works on the genoese Appennine.
